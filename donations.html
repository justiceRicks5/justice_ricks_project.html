Support Our Church

Your generous donations help us continue to spread the Word of God, support our ministries, and serve our community. There are several ways you can give, and we deeply appreciate any contributions you make to further the work of Jones Grove Missionary Baptist Church.

Ways to Give

Online Donations

You can give securely online via our church’s donation portal. Whether it’s a one-time offering or a recurring gift, you can support the church easily from the comfort of your home.

Donation Form

In-Person Giving

You can give during any of our regular worship services. Just place your tithes and offerings in the donation baskets during the offering time. Our ushers will be happy to assist you.

Mail Donations

If you prefer, you can mail your donations directly to the church. Please send checks to:

Jones Grove Missionary Baptist Church
123 Church Street
Louisville, GA 30434

Other Ways to Give

You can also give through other means, such as planned giving, stock donations, or legacy giving. Please contact us for more information on these options.
