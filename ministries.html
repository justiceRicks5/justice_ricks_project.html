Our Ministries

Jones Grove is home to a variety of ministries designed to meet the needs of people from all walks of life. From our Youth Ministry to our Senior Adult Ministry, we believe that everyone has a place to serve and grow. We also actively engage in outreach, both locally and globally, providing support and resources to those in need.

Ministry Programs

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry focuses on nurturing young people in their faith and guiding them through challenges they may face. Activities include Bible study, youth group meetings, and outreach programs. This ministry is a great way for young people to connect and grow spiritually.

Choir

The Jones Grove Choir provides beautiful music during our worship services. Open to members of all ages, our choir ministry brings together individuals who have a passion for singing and glorifying God through music.

Praise Team

Our Praise Team leads worship with contemporary songs, creating a vibrant atmosphere during services. The team consists of talented vocalists and musicians who are dedicated to using their gifts to honor God.
